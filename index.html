How this is measured
  • Window: the previous complete week, bucketed by day-of-week × hour. Voice comes from Amazon Connect, email from Zoho Desk. Phone hours are Central; email hours are the stored (offshore-heavy) time zone — directional, not exact.
  • Inbound volume = total demand offered to the queues (calls offered / emails received). This is whole-client demand and is NOT attributable to a specific BPO — a contact isn't "owned" by a vendor until an agent picks it up. That's why the BPO filter is greyed out on Inbound and only applies to Handled & Gap.
  • Handled = calls answered / emails replied, by BPO — the only metric that splits by vendor (the BPO buttons sum to "All").
  • Gap = Inbound − Handled = unmet demand (abandoned calls / unanswered emails). Shown per hour so you can see when coverage falls short. Gap is a total (not per-BPO), for the same reason Inbound is.
  • Scope: patient inbound only. Back-office / clinical lines (pharmacy, CMA triage, retention, prior-auth, etc.) are excluded. "Other" = patient clients without a built dashboard.
