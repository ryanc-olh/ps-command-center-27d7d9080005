How this dashboard works — data sources, refresh & methods (click to expand)
  • What this is: the internal Patient Support operations command center — de-identified aggregates across all clients & channels. Internal only (contains BPO/agent detail); not client-facing.
  • Data sources: 📞 Voice = Amazon Connect (mirrored call logs, current to the prior day). ✉️ Email = Zoho Desk (mirrored, up to ~1-day lag). Phone reasons & call sentiment = Amazon Connect Contact Lens.
  • Refresh cadence: this command center + the phone Reasons-for-Contact view + per-client email noise rates rebuild daily ~5:15am Mountain; the email Reasons-for-Contact reason-mix rebuilds weekly, Monday ~6:30am MT; the embedded client dashboards refresh every 4 hours. It is an auto-rebuilt file (refreshed on a schedule), not a live stream.
  • Three kinds of number on this page:
    • LIVE — measured, auto-refreshed daily: voice answered / ASA / AHT / abandon / service level; email replies / first-response / one-touch / replies-per-ticket; intraday volumes; deployed agents; phone reasons + sentiment. ("Live" = re-measured on each daily rebuild — a daily batch current to the prior day, not a real-time stream.)
    • MEASURED · STATIC — measured once, not auto-refreshed (a script is re-run to update): the email throughput coefficient (2.1 replies per active agent-hour) and the per-client email real-patient (noise) rates (measured over Jun 22–Jul 12 2026, ~250 emails/client; e.g. Embody 98.8% real-patient, Medvi 85.1%, Other 45.2%).
    • YOUR ASSUMPTION — editable inputs (Capacity tab & the Intraday "Agents needed" bar): Erlang target service-level % & answer-seconds, email SLA-window hours, shrinkage %.
  • Models: Voice staffing uses Erlang C — the contact-center industry-standard queueing model for staffing to a service-level target (full formula + rationale on the Intraday tab and the Capacity tab). Email staffing uses an asynchronous throughput / labor-balance floor. Call sentiment is AWS Contact Lens. Email noise is AI-classified (two passes + tiebreak).
  • Date windows (each tab also shows its own 📅 window): Live Ops = latest complete week for contacts / email / sentiment / BPO, but voice service level, abandon & clients-below-SLA = trailing 7 days; By-BPO scorecard & By-Client = latest complete week; voice SLA tab = trailing 7 days; Week-over-week = 6 complete weeks + current partial (*); Intraday & capacity = previous complete week; phone reasons = latest complete week; email reasons = previous complete week (~100-ticket sample/client).
  • Time zones: refresh times are Mountain; voice & phone volumes are reported in Central; email in its stored time zone.
  • Email is patient-only: every email volume/demand figure is scaled by each client's measured real-patient rate to strip automated/marketing/vendor/system noise; reply & response-time metrics are not scaled, and voice is not adjusted.
