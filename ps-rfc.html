How these numbers are pulled
  • Email reasons (all clients): each week we take a random sample of 100 tickets per client from the previous complete week and read every ticket's actual message to classify why the patient contacted us. We don't use Zoho's built-in "category" field — it's blank on ~all email — so reasons are read from the real text.
  • What the % means: a reason's share of that client's 100-ticket sample (so columns total ~100%). It's a representative sample, not every ticket — directional for the mix, not an exact count.
  • Refresh: automatically every Monday. De-identified aggregates only — no patient info.
  • Phone reasons: a one-time manual tagging exercise (Code Yellow, week of Jun 14), calls only — not yet auto-refreshed. Phone and email mixes differ, so compare within a channel.
  • "Other" client: patient clients that don't have their own dashboard yet. ~71% of it is non-patient noise (vendor/marketing/system), so treat its patient reasons as a thin slice.
